# BuybackCalculator
A contract calculator for eve

Go to https://ladycailin.github.io/BuybackCalculator/ to get started. First, select a list of items that you are
interested in buying back. Then, fill in all the prices you wish to pay per item (you can leave ones that you
aren't going to buy back blank, and they will be filled with 0's.) Create the link, and then you'll get a long
link which you can share with your corp. The corp members will go to this page, then paste in their inventory,
and get a total list that they can easily figure the contract price from.
